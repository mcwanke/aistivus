### Data Privacy

AIstivus is designed as a local, self-hosted tool. By default:

- All data is stored on your local machine in a SQLite database
- No data is transmitted to external services unless you explicitly configure a cloud LLM provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.)
- When using cloud LLM providers, your job description text and personal context document (`jobsearch.md`) are transmitted to that provider's API and subject to their privacy policy and terms of service

**If you configure a cloud LLM provider, you are responsible for reviewing that provider's privacy policy and terms of service before transmitting any personal or sensitive information.**
